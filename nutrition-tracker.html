Welcome to the Local Nutrition Tracker

This tracker is designed to run entirely on your device. No accounts are required and no information is stored online. All data is saved locally in your browser until you export a backup.

Quick Start

  1. Open Profile / Goals and enter your age, sex, height, weight, goal weight, activity level, and desired weight-loss plan.
  2. Select today's date on the Main Tracker.
  3. Use the Food page to add foods, supplements, meals, or recipes.
  4. Use the Exercise page to record physical activity and calories burned.
  5. Review your totals on the Main Tracker.
  6. Visit Stats to compare your intake with nutritional targets.
  7. Export a backup regularly.

Main Tracker

  • Displays daily calorie intake, net calories, estimated weight change, and your logged daily scale weight.
  • Shows complete protein and collagen protein separately.
  • Lists food entries and lets you assign them to custom groups.
  • Displays exercise entries for the selected date.
  • Allows exporting, importing, clearing the current day, or resetting all app data.

Food Page

  • Add foods from the built-in database on Today's Menu.
  • Specify servings, then group foods after they are added.
  • Create custom foods with your own nutritional information on Add Custom Food to Database.
  • Custom foods are stored locally and remain available until deleted or reset.

Exercise Page

  • Log exercise duration and calories burned.
  • Uses CDC reference activities to estimate calorie expenditure.
  • Exercise calories are subtracted when calculating net calories and deficits.

Food List

  • Browse foods organized by category.
  • Includes meals, supplements, fruits, vegetables, proteins, grains, and more.
  • Displays serving sizes and nutrition information.

Recipes

The Recipes page stores recipes and allows them to be added directly to the current day.

  • View ingredients and preparation instructions.
  • Add the selected recipe directly to your daily log.
  • Review nutrition information associated with each recipe.

Recipes currently included may include:

  • Tuna Stuffed Bell Peppers
  • Air Fryer Cod
  • Quiche (8-egg version)
  • Chicken Mushroom Tortellini
  • Pho – Beef
  • Einkorn Bread variations
  • Dessert recipes added through tracker updates
  • Cucumber - Fermenteds (2% Brine)
  • Additional custom recipes saved by the user

Daily Nutrition Breakdown

  • Shows each nutrient consumed during the day.
  • Identifies which foods contributed to each nutrient total.
  • Useful for identifying nutrient deficiencies or excesses.

Stats

  • Displays macro summaries.
  • Compares intake against nutritional targets.
  • Separates Vitamin A forms for safety awareness.
  • Tracks Vitamin D3 separately from other Vitamin D sources.
  • Tracks Vitamin K1 separately from Vitamin K2 when available.
  • Separates collagen protein from complete protein.

Profile / Goals

  • Stores your personal profile information.
  • Calculates maintenance calories using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation unless a manual maintenance value is entered.
  • Supports weight-loss, maintenance, and weight-gain plans.

Shopping Lists

  • Select multiple days of logged foods.
  • Generate a consolidated grocery list.
  • Combine matching foods into total quantities.
  • Print shopping lists for meal preparation.

Backups

  • Export Backup: Save all tracker information to a file.
  • Import Backup: Restore previously exported data.
  • Regular backups are recommended before major edits.

Tips

  • Review Stats regularly to monitor nutritional adequacy.
  • Use custom foods for products not already included.
  • Use recipes to simplify frequent meal logging.
  • Export backups periodically to protect your data.
